Strategy Guide

Blast Off is a game of 99% luck, but 1% skill. This means that if you play in a strategy-oblivious way, you'll not do too bad. But with a good enough strategy, it's actually possible to overcome the house odds, and even make a profit.

Here's how it works:

Instabust

Right off the bat, there's a 2.77% chance of the game instantly busting. If this happens, no one can win anything. This means freebitcoins.com has an expected return of 2.77% * playersInGame * tableAmount.

House Edge

House edge scales between 0 and 1%. see: odds and calculator.. Our calculator does not factor the 2.77% instant bust.
